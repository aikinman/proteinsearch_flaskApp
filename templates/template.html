A tool from the Cembrowski Lab at the University of British Columbia

Protein Search is an automated code-free tool for scientists and clinicians to search through human antibodies labeling neurons and glia in the brain.

Protein Search is directly compatible with Seurat's "FindMarkers()" function and returns a dataframe of antibodies based on information from the Human Protein Atlas
